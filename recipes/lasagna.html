Lasagna

Home

Delicious layers of pasta, meat, and cheese!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and onion, and sauté until softened.
  4. Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with a beaten egg and a pinch of salt.
  7. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of noodles, then a layer of ricotta mixture, and a sprinkle of mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce topped with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  9. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
